Short-Form Trailer Edit

Footage from Game Changer | Music: “It’s the Hard Knock Life

Project Overview

This short-form edit repurposes footage from Game Changer, a Dropout comedy game show where contestants compete in unpredictable games without knowing the rules in advance.

Editing Strategy

To maintain engagement in a fast-scroll environment, the edit prioritizes tight pacing and rapid comedic payoff. Reaction shots, punchlines, and visual beats were selected to build momentum while preserving narrative clarity.

Post-Production Workflow

Footage was reviewed and organized to isolate moments with strong contestant reactions and comedic delivery. Clips were grouped by scene and narrative function, allowing for efficient iteration while refining the structure of the edit.

The project was edited in Adobe Premiere Pro, with pacing adjustments and graphics integration completed during the refinement stage to maintain narrative clarity and visual consistency.

Impact

The video generated strong engagement on TikTok, reaching 70.8K+ views.

The edit also received direct interaction from Dropout leadership: CEO Sam Reich liked, commented on, and reposted the video, indicating positive reception from the creators of the original show.

Rhythm-Based Character Montage

Footage from Supernatural | Music: “Another One Bites the Dust” – Queen

Project Overview

This project is a music-driven character narrative montage highlighting the chemistry and action dynamics of the central characters from the fantasy series Supernatural.

The goal of the edit was to create a fast-paced narrative montage that captures the tone and personality of the show while emphasizing the characters’ teamwork, humour, and action-driven storytelling.

Editing Strategy

The song “Another One Bites the Dust” was selected to reflect the show’s long-standing use of classic rock in key narrative moments and recap sequences. Its driving bassline provides a strong rhythmic foundation for pacing the montage.

The edit was structured around the rhythm of the track, with cuts and transitions aligned to musical beats and lyrical cues. Scene selection prioritizes action moments alongside comedic character interactions to reinforce the dynamic between the characters while maintaining narrative flow.

Post-Production Workflow

Footage was organized into structured bins by character and scene type to streamline scene selection and support efficient iteration during editing.

The project was edited in Adobe Premiere Pro, with color adjustments and audio balancing applied to maintain visual consistency and clear sound levels across clips sourced from different episodes

Impact

The video generated strong engagement on YouTube, reaching 100K+ views.
